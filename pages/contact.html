Frequently Asked Questions

How does ShipIt work?

ShipIt connects customers with individual shippers through an innovative bidding system. Customers post their shipment details, and verified shippers place competitive bids. Customers then choose the best offer for their needs

How do I become a shipper on ShipIt?

To become a shipper on ShipIt, you need to sign up for an account, complete our verification process, and agree to our terms of service. Once approved, you can start bidding on available shipments.

Is my payment secure?

Yes, all payments on ShipIt are secure. We use industry-standard encryption and secure payment gateways to ensure your financial information is protected.
